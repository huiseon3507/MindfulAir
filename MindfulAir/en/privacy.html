Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

MindfulAir ("we", "our", or "the Company") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with applicable privacy laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information

We process personal information for the following purposes only. Personal information will not be used for any purposes other than those listed below. If the purpose changes, we will obtain separate consent as required by applicable privacy laws.

  • App Service Provision: Storing breathing meditation records and cloud synchronization
  • Customer Support: Responding to inquiries and providing technical support

2. Personal Information Collection Items and Methods

2.1 Collection Items

  • Required Information: Meditation records
  • Automatically Collected Information: Device information (OS version, device model), app usage statistics, advertising identifier (IDFA)

2.2 Collection Methods

  • Using the app with anonymous login
  • Through automated log collection tools

3. Personal Information Retention and Use Period

We retain personal information for the period required by law or the period agreed upon when collecting your personal information.

  • User Information: Until account deletion (except where retention is required by law)
  • Meditation Records: Deleted within 30 days after account deletion
  • App Usage Logs: 1 year from collection date

4. Outsourcing of Personal Information Processing

We outsource personal information processing tasks to the following parties for smooth service provision:

  • Supabase: Data storage and management
  • Google (Firebase, AdMob): App analytics, push notifications, advertising services

5. Rights of Users

You may request access to, correction of, deletion of, or suspension of processing your personal information at any time by contacting us via email at huiseon.apps@gmail.com.

6. Destruction of Personal Information

We promptly destroy personal information when the retention period expires or the processing purpose is achieved.

Electronic files are permanently deleted using irrecoverable methods.

7. Measures to Ensure Personal Information Security

We manage personal information through trusted cloud services such as Supabase, Google Firebase, and Google AdMob. Each service provider applies security measures in accordance with industry standards.

8. Privacy Officer

Privacy Officer

  • Email: huiseon.apps@gmail.com
  • Department: MindfulAir Operations Team

You may contact us at the above address for inquiries, complaints, or grievances related to personal information.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of January 22, 2026. If there are additions, deletions, or corrections to the content due to changes in laws and policies, we will notify users through in-app announcements at least 7 days before the changes take effect.

10. Reporting and Consulting on Personal Information Infringement

If you need to report or consult about personal information infringement, you may contact the following organizations:

  • Personal Information Infringement Report Center: (Without area code) 118 (privacy.kisa.or.kr)
  • Personal Information Dispute Mediation Committee: 1833-6972 (kopico.go.kr)
  • Supreme Prosecutors' Office Cyber Investigation Division: (Without area code) 1301 (spo.go.kr)
  • National Police Agency Cyber Safety Bureau: (Without area code) 182 (cyberbureau.police.go.kr)
